Consignment

We are happy to list and sell a watch on your behalf!

How it works:

  1. Using the form below, supply us with the information we need to accurately evaluate your watch.
  2. We receive your submission and evaluate your timepiece.
  3. Our team will send you a consignment agreement via docusign detailing our terms and conditions.
  4. Upon receiving your signature, we will email you a prepaid shipping label to send over your watch and contents to us.
  5. When it sells, you get paid!
