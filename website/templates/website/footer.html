About us

We're a group of watch lovers who started Delta Timepieces back in 2020. We were tired of seeing people get ripped off by crazy markups at traditional jewelry stores. So, we decided to shake things up and make awesome timepieces more affordable for everyone.

Also, we're not affiliated with any big brands like Rolex or Omega. We're just a team of enthusiasts who want to share our passion for watches with the world. We source authentic timepieces and sell them to you at unbeatable prices. No fancy marketing gimmicks or inflated prices here - just great watches at fair prices.
